Privacy Policy — TextyFeed

Last updated: March 2026

TextyFeed ("the app") is a language learning application developed by an independent developer. This privacy policy explains how the app handles your data.

Data Collection

TextyFeed does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data. No account or registration is required to use the app.

Data Stored Locally

The following data is stored exclusively on your device and never sent to any server:

  • Your flashcard deck (words and translations you have saved)
  • App settings (theme, native language preference)
  • Custom RSS sources you have added
  • Daily fetch count (resets every 24 hours)

Internet Access

The app uses your internet connection to:

  • Fetch article content from URLs you provide
  • Load RSS news feeds you select
  • Translate words via Google Translate public API

These requests are made directly from your device to the respective services. TextyFeed does not route your data through any intermediate server.

Third-Party Services

The app uses Google Play Billing to process subscription payments. Payment data is handled entirely by Google and is subject to Google's Privacy Policy. TextyFeed does not receive or store any payment information.

Children's Privacy

TextyFeed is intended for users aged 13 and older. The app does not knowingly collect data from children under 13.

Changes to This Policy

If this privacy policy changes, the updated version will be posted at this URL. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the new policy.
